How It Started

It started with a bad funeral. The kind with fluorescent lighting, a generic playlist, and a room full of people who knew the person deserved better.

We thought: what if we could help people plan celebrations that actually feel like the person being celebrated? What if the planning itself could be joyful?

So we found a shotgun house in the Marigny, filled it with velvet furniture and neon signs, built a cocktail bar, and started helping people plan the party of a lifetime. Their lifetime.

The Space

A converted shotgun house in the Marigny neighborhood of New Orleans. Velvet, neon, Polaroids, and a cocktail bar. It feels like someone's impossibly cool living room — because that is exactly what it used to be.

What We Believe

Death Is Not the Enemy

It is the one experience we all share. We believe talking about it openly makes living richer, not scarier.

Your Story, Your Way

No templates, no defaults, no standard packages. Every celebration starts from scratch, because every person is one of a kind.

Joy Is the Point

Grief and joy are not opposites. The best celebrations hold both at once — and that is what makes them real.
