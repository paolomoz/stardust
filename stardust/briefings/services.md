# Intent
Show visitors exactly what we offer and make each service feel tangible, approachable, and worth investing in. Move them from "this is interesting" to "I want to book this."

# Audience
Visitors who've bought into the concept (from the homepage or word of mouth) and want to understand the specifics. Mix of self-planners (planning their own) and loving organizers (planning for someone else).

# Key Messages
1. Three distinct services: Living Wakes, Death Party Planning, and Grief Support — each with its own personality.
2. Every detail is yours to decide: music, food, dress code, readings, cocktails, the works.
3. We guide the process so you can focus on what matters — celebrating a life well lived.

# Calls to Action
- Primary: "Book a Consultation"
- Secondary: "Learn More" (per service)

# Tone
Clear and confident. Still warm and witty, but dial down the irreverence slightly — people are making a real decision here. Clarity is kindness.
