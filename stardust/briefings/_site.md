# Purpose
Ferro & Fiamma's website tells the story of a wood-fired Neapolitan pizzeria in Brooklyn that is obsessive about sourcing and craft. It makes people hungry, builds trust through transparency, and drives visits and orders.

# Navigation
- Home
- Our Menu

# Shared Messaging
Tagline: "Fire and Iron. Since Brooklyn."
Value prop: Wood-fired Neapolitan pizza made with 72-hour sourdough, San Marzano DOP tomatoes, and local fior di latte. No shortcuts.

# Content Hierarchy
Homepage is the front door — it sells the feeling and drives to the menu or visit. Our Menu is the conversion page — what we serve and why it matters. About Us builds the emotional connection — our story, our sourcing, our people.
