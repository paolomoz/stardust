# Purpose
Good Grief & Co.'s website introduces a death-positive celebration-of-life planning studio in New Orleans. It normalizes planning your own funeral, living wake, or "death party" as a joyful, empowering act. The site must feel like walking into the studio itself: warm, bold, irreverent, and alive.

# Shared Messaging
Tagline: "Plan your party. Yes, that one."
Value proposition: We help you design the send-off you actually want — while you're still here to argue about the playlist.

# Content Hierarchy
Homepage is the front door — emotional hook + credibility + clear path to services. Services is the conversion page — detailed offerings with booking CTAs. About builds trust and human connection — the team, the space, the philosophy.
