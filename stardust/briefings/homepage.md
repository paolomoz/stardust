# Intent
Make visitors hungry and curious within seconds. Convey craft, warmth, and authenticity. The one action: explore the menu or plan a visit.

# Audience
Mix of local Brooklyn residents who stumbled on us, food enthusiasts who found us via Instagram or blogs, and returning regulars checking hours or the menu. Mindset: hungry, curious, or habitual.

# Key Messages
1. We make wood-fired Neapolitan pizza with 72-hour sourdough — the real thing.
2. Our sourcing is obsessive: San Marzano DOP, local fior di latte, small-batch olive oil.
3. We're a neighborhood spot in Brooklyn. Come as you are.

# Calls to Action
- Primary: "View Our Menu"
- Secondary: "Find Us"

# Tone
Confident and inviting. Lead with sensory details — the heat of the oven, the char on the crust. Short punchy sentences. Let the food speak.
