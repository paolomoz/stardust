# Intent
Build trust and emotional connection. Show the humans behind the brand, the story of the space, and the philosophy that drives everything. Make visitors feel like they already know us.

# Audience
Visitors who want to understand the people and values before committing. They're checking for authenticity — are these people for real? Can I trust them with something this personal?

# Key Messages
1. We started Good Grief because we believe the way we say goodbye should feel as personal as the way we lived.
2. Our space is a converted shotgun house in the Marigny — velvet, neon, Polaroids, and a cocktail bar. It feels like someone's impossibly cool living room.
3. Our team has planned 200+ celebrations and every single one was different, because every single person is different.

# Calls to Action
- Primary: "Get in Touch"
- Secondary: "Visit the Studio"

# Tone
Warm and personal. This is the most tender page — let the sincerity show. Still confident, still us, but the humor takes a back seat to genuine human connection.
