# Intent
Tell our story in a way that deepens trust and emotional connection. Visitors should feel they know us — where we came from, what drives us, and why we do things the way we do. The page builds loyalty and makes regulars feel part of something.

# Audience
People who already like the food and want to know the story behind it. Also food writers, press, and potential partners looking for our narrative.

# Key Messages
1. We started because we couldn't find real Neapolitan pizza in Brooklyn — so we built an oven and learned.
2. Our sourcing is a point of pride: every ingredient has a story and a named supplier.
3. This is a neighborhood place. The community is as important as the crust.

- Primary: "View Our Menu"
- Secondary: "Visit Us"

# Tone
Storytelling — personal, a little nostalgic, deeply passionate. Longer sentences are okay here. This is where we open up.
