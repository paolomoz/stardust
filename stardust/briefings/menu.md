# Intent
Show what we serve and make every item sound irresistible. Visitors should leave either placing an order or planning a visit. The menu itself is proof of our craft — fewer items, each one exceptional.

# Audience
Hungry people deciding what to eat. Some know Neapolitan pizza, some don't. They want to see what's available, understand what makes it special, and find prices.

# Key Messages
1. Every pizza starts with 72-hour sourdough and ends in a 900°F wood-fired oven.
2. Our menu is tight — we do a few things and we do them right.
3. Ingredients matter: every item lists where key ingredients come from.

# Calls to Action
- Primary: "Order Now"
- Secondary: "Visit Us"

# Tone
Direct and appetizing. Menu descriptions have personality but never obscure what the dish actually is. Concrete details: name the ingredients, the technique, the origin.
