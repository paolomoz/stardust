How It Started

It started with a bad funeral. The kind with fluorescent lighting, a generic playlist, and a room full of people who knew the person deserved better.

We thought: what if we could help people plan celebrations that actually feel like the person being celebrated? What if the planning itself could be joyful?

So we found a shotgun house in the Marigny, filled it with velvet furniture and neon signs, built a cocktail bar, and started helping people plan the party of a lifetime.
